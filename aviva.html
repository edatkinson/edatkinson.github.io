Aviva Insurance Optimisation Project

This project focused on optimizing repair garage locations for Aviva Insurance to minimize the time for vehicle recoveries. The solution involved using various data-driven techniques, including:

  • K-means clustering: To group data points based on geographical and logistical factors.
  • Dijkstra’s Algorithm: To calculate the shortest paths for vehicle recoveries.
  • Louvain’s Method: For community detection, helping to identify clusters of high-demand areas.

The result was a significant reduction in average recovery time, leading to improved customer satisfaction and operational efficiency for Aviva.
